Table --- BoxModels

  • BoxModelID -- Int32 (key)
        Contains the unique identifier on each row of the BoxModels table
  • DBCommand -- DBCommandEnum (int)
        Identify the database command for local database sync
  • InfrastructureTVItemID -- Int32
        Link to the TVItems table representing the infrastructure
  • Discharge_m3_day -- Double (min=0) (max=10000)
        Discharge (m3/d) spilled by the infrastructure
  • Depth_m -- Double (min=0) (max=1000)
        Average depth (m) of water in the vicinity of the spill
  • Temperature_C -- Double (min=-15) (max=40)
        Temperature (deg C) of water in the vicinity of the spill
  • Dilution -- Int32 (min=0) (max=10000000)
        Dilution (MPN / 100 mL) level to uptain in the vicinity of the spill
  • DecayRate_per_day -- Double (min=0) (max=100)
        Average decay rate (/day) of the fecal coliform colonies
  • FCUntreated_MPN_100ml -- Int32 (min=0) (max=10000000)
        Fecal coliform concentration of untreaded waste water (MPN / 100 mL)
  • FCPreDisinfection_MPN_100ml -- Int32 (min=0) (max=10000000)
        Fecal coliform concentration of pre-disinfected waste water (MPN / 100 mL)
  • Concentration_MPN_100ml -- Int32 (min=0) (max=10000000)
        Fecal coliform concentration objective after dilution (MPN / 100 mL)
  • T90_hour -- Double (min=0)
        Time (hour) required for 90 % of the fecal coliform colonies to die off
  • DischargeDuration_hour -- Double (min=0) (max=24)
        Discharge duration (hour/day)
  • LastUpdateDate_UTC -- DateTime
        Contains the date of the last update in UTC
  • LastUpdateContactTVItemID -- Int32
        Contact TVItemID who made the last changes
